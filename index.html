About Us

Mulungi Coffee Nursery Bed, located in Busunju, Mityana District, is dedicated to producing high-quality coffee seedlings. We take pride in our healthy, disease-resistant cuttings and sustainable nursery practices.

We provide strong, healthy coffee cuttings — ready to thrive in any coffee-growing region.

We provide professionally grown, healthy coffee seedlings — rooted under proper shade and ready for planting.

Your order will be delivered to your doorstep, anywhere.
